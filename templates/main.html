What is this research about?


Through the result of this task, we can classify negative emotions of Contempt, Anger and Disgust (CAD) in three different cultures of North American, Persian and Filipino. We also want to find the common facial expressions within each emotion. With your aid, robots and intelligent agents in future will be able to identify whether people are statisfied with their interaction or not.
